What is Delta?

Delta is a decentralized global digital currency project that requires long-term development, as well as a currency application ecosystem.

What is the direction of Delta?

Humans can use Delta to jointly build a universal, fair, secure, fast, and green new digital currency.
And provide various effective application scenarios for these digital currencies and previous cryptocurrencies.

Who is the Delta development team?

The early development of Delta was led by anonymous programmer Haida. Later, anyone who agrees with Delta's development vision and development plan and has relevant talents will have the opportunity to become part of the Delta developer team.

What technology does Delta use?

The core of Delta system runs on Internet Computer smart contract platform and is implemented in motoko language. The front-end mobile app is developed and implemented in Flutter.

How to understand Delta mining?

The essence of mining in Delta is a distribution mechanism, which has nothing to do with factors such as hardware computing power and network bandwidth.

What is the difference between DTC and DTCT?

DTC is the abbreviation of Delta Coin, DTCT is the abbreviation of Delta Credit, and DTCT is DTC that has not been activated and released.

What is the total amount of DTC?

DTC is scheduled to have a total amount of 300 billion in the early stage. According to the white paper, after the establishment of the "Governance Committee", it will vote to decide whether to issue more and the issuance plan.

How to get more DTC?

  • 4. Raise funds for ICP fuel

What is a promotion ambassador? How is the team reward calculated?

When you invite others to become members of Delta through your DID, you will automatically become a promotion ambassador.

Every time you directly recommend one person, your own mining rate will increase by 1/3 of the basic rate; every time you indirectly recommend (secondary recommendation) one person, the mining rate will increase by 1/10 of the basic rate.

What is 3-No-Verification?

It means that users do not need a private key, mnemonic, or password to enter the system. It is also called decentralized SMS verification.

Do I need to manage private keys, mnemonics or passwords when using Delta?

Yes, Delta adopts 3-no verification + security circle, and users do not need to manage private keys, mnemonics and remember passwords.

What is a security circle?

The security circle is a verification model based on user social relationships, and this function is completely decentralized. Users add 3-5 trusted people in the social relationship circle to form a security circle, and help them achieve secondary security verification when necessary. The security circle function can effectively increase the difficulty of malicious attacks and reduce the risk of accounts being acquired by others.

What is USDT dual-currency mining?

USDT dual-currency mining means that DTC and USDT are mined at the same time, which is a means of traffic monetization. Link

What is the value of DTC?

The value of DTC is determined by the consensus of all Delta members, and the final price is reflected by the transaction between the buyer and seller of the commodity. Therefore, no one can set a price for DTC.

When will DTC have value? When can I turn DTC into "real money"?

After the user passes KYC, it will be gradually released according to the white paper agreement, and the released DTC can circulate freely.

Can I install Delta app on multiple devices at the same time for mining?

No, anyone can only hold one Delta account for mining, and members who mine repeatedly will be excluded and punished during the KYC stage.

What is Delta multi-chain wallet?

Decentralized multi-chain wallet is one of the core functions built into the Delta system. Delta multi-chain wallet enables users to transfer assets, exchange information and collaborate with other blockchains. It can easily receive and send other public chain assets, and when users manage these assets, they do not need any private keys, mnemonics or passwords. This convenient and secure feature is mainly due to Delta's three-no verification protocol and Internet Computer-based ECDSA threshold signature (Threshold ECDSA signatures) technology.

What is ICP crowdfunding?

It is a way for Delta to raise fuel for smart contract operation. Users can raise ICP fuel tokens and obtain a certain amount of DTC/DTCT.

What is dApp Square?

DApp Square is Delta's decentralized application market, a platform for Delta's online ecosystem construction, and a future-oriented Web3 application center. Any user with development capabilities can develop Delta applications and settle in.

Is Delta an open source project?

Delta has open sourced some sub-projects and will gradually and fully open source them as planned.
